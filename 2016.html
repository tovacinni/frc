>> FRC

2016 was my last robotics season. I was the Team Leader this year, which meant that alongside making sure that we had a competitive robot, I had to worry about a lot of other things like team culture, inclusiveness, student outcome, logistics, and dealing with team politics. This was an inevitable part of a student-led team comprised of 40+ students and 15+ adult volunteers.

This year was definitely the most stressful for me, but I had fun nonetheless.
